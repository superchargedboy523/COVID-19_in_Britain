How can you help stop the spread of Covid-19

  • Stay at home, only going out once a day essentials or an excercise.
  • Wash your hands thouroughly before and after eating food and after going outside.
  • Don't go to meet friends or relatives even if they ask you to.
  • Try not to touch hard surfaces which are open to the public.
